## macOS & Terminal Commands

### `pwd`
*   **Definition:** "Print Working Directory". Shows the exact folder path you are currently looking at in the terminal.
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn macOS](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-macos/README.md)

### `ls`
*   **Definition:** "List". Lists all the files and folders inside the directory you are currently in.
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn macOS](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-macos/README.md)

### Spotlight Search (`Cmd + Space`)
*   **Definition:** A quick search bar on Mac that lets you find files, launch apps, calculate math, or look up information.
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn macOS](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-macos/README.md)

## TV Production & Filmmaking Terms

### Low Angle Shot
*   **Definition:** A shot taken from a low position looking up at the subject. It makes the subject appear strong, powerful, or intimidating.
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn TV Production](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-tv-production/README.md)

### Slugline (Scene Heading)
*   **Definition:** A bold line at the start of a screenwriting scene that denotes whether it is inside or outside (`INT.` vs `EXT.`), the location name, and the time of day (`DAY` vs `NIGHT`).
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn Screenwriting](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-screenwriting/README.md)

### J-Cut
*   **Definition:** An editing cut where the audio of the next scene starts playing before you see the video transition. Named because the visual blocks on the editing timeline resemble a "J".
*   **Tutorial Path:** [Learn Video Editing](file:///Users/annashuvalova/tutorial/learn-video-editing/README.md)
